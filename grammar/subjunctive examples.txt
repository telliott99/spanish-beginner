Yo quiero que tú limpies el baño

Notice:
- two clauses
- a relative pronoun (que)
- two verbs, one WEIRDO

WEIRDO = wishes, emotions, impersonal exp, 
         doubt/denial and ojalá

Esperamos que cocines bien
Me alegro de que sonrías

In the present:
ar:  e es e emos en
er:  a as a amos an
ir:  a as a amos an

Pretty straightforward, just switch vowels.
