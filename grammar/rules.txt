The personal "a":
whenever the direct object of a verb is a person or a pet, that object must be preceded by the Spanish personal a.

- Ellos ayudan a los estudiantes mucho.
- Èl camina a su perro en el parque.

The passive voice is formed with "se" and the third-person singular or plural of the verb.

- No se vende  eso aqui.
- Sì, se llaman Español in Mèxico.
